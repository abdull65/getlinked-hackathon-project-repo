Judging Criteria
Key attributes

  • Innovation and creativity: Evaluate the uniqueness and creativity of the solution, Consider wheather it addresses a real-world problem in a novel way or introduces innovative features.
  • Functionality: Asscss how well the solution works. Does it perform its intended unctions effectively and without major issues? judges would consider the completeness and robustness of the solution.
  • Impact and Relevance: Determine the potential impact of the solution in the real world. Does it address a significant problem, and is it relevant to the target audience? judges would assess the potenital social, economic, or enviromental benefits.
  • Technical Complexity: Evaluate the technical sophistication of the solution. Judges would consider the complexity of the code, the use of advanced technology and algorithms, and the scalability of the solution.
  • Adherence to Hackathon Rules: Judges will Ensure that the team adherer to the rules and guidelines of the hackathon, including deadlines of specific technologies or APIs, and any other competition-specific requirements.
